Our purpose

We want to favor the diffusion of sustainable solutions at large scale, by sharing successful ideas and by gathering motivated students, companies, and labs.

To this purpose, we organize this competition so that students', labs', and companies' projects can be showcased, shared, and discussed about.

The Competition

The competition aims at assessing the potential of the promising solutions under development. It is subdivided into the following categories :

  • Direct air carbon capture
  • Carbon Utilization or storage
  • Batteries and other energy storage methods
  • Low-tech solutions

    • The assessment of the potential of each project will take into account the performances, the feasibility, the scalability, the financial invewstments needed...
